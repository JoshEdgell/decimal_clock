Clock Converter

What's a decmial clock? A clock that has only 10 hours in a day, each comprised of a hundred 100-second minutes.

But that's 100,000 seconds - there are only 86,400 seconds in a day! Seconds are shorter in "decimal time" - about 15% shorter

OK...so why's there an app for it? Because I felt like making an app to demonstrate the concept of a decimal clock.

Couldn't you just make an app that displayed decimal time? I could have. But there'd be no way for someone to see how quickly seconds tick by in a different time unit when compared to our own.

OK...so why didn't you just show decimal time against our own? Again, I could have. But where's the fun in that? I mean, haven't you ever wondered what a day would look like if there were 3 divisions that lasted 7 hours each and each of the 94 minutes was 57 seconds long? OK, That's a bit much. In short, though, I just thought the math would be cool, and the challenge of coding it out would be fun.

So why'd you include a stopwatches and a timers? Just felt like it, I guess. Again, I figured the math would be kind of interesting, and that I might learn a few new CSS tricks in displaying them as a rotating cube.
